Simply-Typed Lambda Calculus

This is the simply-typed lambda calculus with integers and addition, based on the extensible language in "Sound type-dependent syntactic language extension". Of particular interest in this example is the proof of progress, particularly the way canonical form lemmas are proven.

  • Host language: Simply-typed lambda calculus with integers and addition proving uniqueness of evaluation, type preservation, and progress
  • Let extension: New syntax for let expressions
  • Pair extension: New syntax for pair expressions
